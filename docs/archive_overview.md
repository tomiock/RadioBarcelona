# Radio Barcelona Archive — Overview

Archive of digitised Radio Barcelona (E.A.J.-1) documents, 1924–1953, held at the UAB digital library.

## Directory structure

The archive is organised under `/data/storage/datasets/RadioBarcelona/pdf_images/` with two naming conventions that reflect the archive's evolution:

| Period | Format | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| 1924–1939 | `guiradbcn_a{year}m{month}` | `guiradbcn_a1931m10` |
| 1940–1953 | `guiradbcn_a{year}m{month}d{day}` | `guiradbcn_a1944m1d3` |

The shift from monthly to daily directories at 1940 reflects the structural change in the archive itself: early years contain one bound booklet per month; later years have individual day folders because each day generated multiple document pages.

The archive grows ~70× from the 1930s baseline to peak years (1951–1952). The 1942–1943 jump (1.5K → 7K) marks the introduction of full radio script transcriptions alongside programme sheets.

## Key observations

- **1927**: completely absent from the digitised collection.
- **1939**: only 6 months, corresponding to the end of the Spanish Civil War (March 1939).
- **1940**: structural reset — sparse daily programme sheets, institutional typewriter format, UAB watermark appears consistently.
- **1942–1943**: volume inflection. Days start generating multiple pages (programme sheet + script transcripts).
- **1953**: archive appears to end mid-year (261 day-directories vs 360+ in prior years).
- **Duplicate scans**: `1953_1.png` and `1953_2.png` in the handpicked sample set are byte-for-byte identical (same file size: 8 792 905 bytes). Likely a double-sided or duplicate physical copy scanned twice. Run deduplication before any training set construction covering 1953.

## Average pages per day (1940–1953)

Grows steadily from ~2 images/day in 1940 to ~70–100 images/day by 1950–1952, with high variance: some days have 150+ pages (long drama scripts), others still just one programme sheet.
